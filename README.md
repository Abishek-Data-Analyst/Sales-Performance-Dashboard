# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard

## 🎯 Project Objective

Designed an interactive Power BI dashboard to transform raw retail data into actionable business insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor KPIs, optimize sales strategies, improve customer experience, and enhance overall business performance.

---

## ⚙️ Process

### 📥 Data Collection

Gathered raw data from Excel and SQL sources, including sales, customers, products, and orders.

### 🧹 Data Cleaning

Removed duplicates, handled missing values, and standardized data formats to ensure data accuracy and consistency.

### 🔄 Data Transformation

Utilized Power Query to clean, reshape, and create calculated columns such as revenue and delivery status.

### 🔗 Data Integration

Merged and consolidated multiple datasets into a unified analytical structure.

### 🏗️ Data Modeling

Built relationships between tables and designed a star schema for efficient reporting and analysis.

### 📐 DAX Calculations

Developed key measures including Total Sales, Average Rating, Delayed Orders, and other business KPIs.

### 📈 Dashboard Creation

Created an interactive Power BI dashboard featuring KPI cards, charts, slicers, filters, and Top-N analysis visuals.

### 💡 Insights & Reporting

Analyzed trends, identified business opportunities, and presented findings through dynamic visualizations.

## 🔍 Key Insights

1. Identified top-performing products and categories contributing the highest revenue
2. Analyzed seasonal sales trends to determine peak business periods
3. Evaluated customer purchasing behavior and repeat order patterns
4. Detected delayed orders to highlight operational bottlenecks
5. Assessed product ratings and customer feedback to measure satisfaction
6. Identified underperforming products and regions for improvement opportunities
7. Enabled real-time KPI tracking for sales, profit, and order volume
